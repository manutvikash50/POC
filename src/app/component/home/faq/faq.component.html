Faq

What is the difference between hiring agents from the marketplace and
bringing my own agents?

When you hire agents from our marketplace, you don't have to pay the per seat fee —
you pay them their hourly rate for the work they complete. If you already have
employees or volunteers who will make calls for you on Overpass, the fee is $24/
month per seat.
